Frequently Asked Questions

What is link building vs backlinks?

Link building refers to the process of acquiring backlinks from external websites to your own. Backlinks are the actual hyperlinks present on other websites that direct users to your site.

Is link building free?

Link building can be both free and paid. Organic methods like guest posting and content marketing can be free, while paid methods include sponsored content or paid link placements.

How much should I pay for link building?

The cost of link building varies depending on factors such as the quality of websites, type of backlinks, and the reputation of the service provider. Prices can range from a few dollars to hundreds or even thousands for premium placements.
